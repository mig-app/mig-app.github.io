privacy

last updated · may 2026

mig is a russian-language vocabulary app that runs entirely on your device. it collects no personal information, no usage analytics, no diagnostic data, and makes no network connections of any kind.

all vocabulary sets and user preferences (display mode, words-per-minute, romanization toggle, etc.) are stored locally in your device's app storage. this data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

the app contains no advertising, no third-party sdks, no telemetry, and no tracking of any kind. when you delete mig from your device, all associated data is removed.

what mig does not do

  • does not require an account or sign-in
  • does not request access to contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
  • does not communicate with any server
  • does not share any data with third parties
  • does not use cookies (it isn't a website)

children

mig is rated 4+ and contains no content unsuitable for children. it collects no data from any user regardless of age.

contact

questions about this policy or about mig in general can be sent to the support page.
