МИГ MiG

russian, a little at a time

mig (миг, russian for "an instant") is a russian vocabulary app built around short, focused practice. read a set at speed, flip through the cards, say each word aloud and hear how close you got, or just listen while mig reads it to you. a few minutes at a time, on your device.

a growing library of curated vocabulary across a1, a2, and b1 levels: family, food, transport, weather, emotions, technology, abstract concepts, and more, plus a set of everyday sentences.

download on the app store

one word, many angles

seeing a word once rarely makes it stick. mig comes at it from four directions: read it at speed, turn it over on a card, say it out loud, and hear it read to you while you walk. the app quietly tracks which words have stuck, and can narrow any set down to the ones that have not.

practice modes

  • блиц flash: read a set from 50 to 1000 words per minute
  • карточки flashcards: flip through, tap the speaker to hear it
  • повторяй repeat aloud: say each word, scored on your device
  • слушай listen: mig reads the set aloud, looping, screen off

on the web

the practice modes, in the browser, no install. progress and streaks live in the app.

блиц flash · speed reading
the mode this app started as: read a set at 50 to 1000 words per minute, russian first, translation on the beat.
повторяй repeat after me
hear a russian word or sentence, say it back, and get your pronunciation scored. words and phrases, in the browser.
карточки flashcards
review a ready-made mig set, or make and import your own deck. tap to flip, tap the speaker to hear the word.

about

mig is built by devin dyson, a graduate student at boston university who started learning russian and wanted a tool that didn't exist. it's free, ad-free, and tracks nothing.
