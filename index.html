МИГ MiG

russian, a little at a time

mig (миг, russian for "an instant") is a russian vocabulary app built around short, focused practice. read a set at speed, flip through the cards, say each word aloud and hear how close you got, or just listen while mig reads it to you. a few minutes at a time, on your device.

a growing library of curated vocabulary across a1, a2, and b1 levels: family, food, transport, weather, emotions, technology, abstract concepts, and more, plus a set of everyday sentences.

about

mig is built by devin dyson, a graduate student at boston university who started learning russian and wanted a simple and free tool to practice russian vocabulary. it's ad-free, and tracks nothing.

get mig

mig runs on iphone. the sets, the four modes, your streak and the word of the day widget are all in the app, and it works offline.
