мигMiG

a speed-reading vocabulary app for russian

mig (миг, russian for "an instant") drills russian vocabulary at speed. paired, learning, and russian-only flash modes, from 50 to 1000 words per minute. clean. quiet. on your device.

thirty curated vocabulary sets across a1, a2, and b1 levels: family, food, transport, weather, emotions, technology, abstract concepts, and more. around 1,500 words covering the core territory of beginner and intermediate russian.

why mig

built for recognition speed

flashcard apps optimize for memorization. mig optimizes for rapid recognition: the skill you actually need when reading a sign, scanning a menu, or catching a word in conversation. five minutes a day, watching words go by faster than you thought you could read them.
